The designers

Our designers are independant self-employed people with a vision.
This brand was started with the simple idea to help everyone make their ideas and designs seen and known.

How it works?

We provide high quality ethically sourced materials to bring your design to life. Just contact us and we can help you turn your designs into products. You can join the team or just design for us whenever you feel like it. You set the terms.

What we mean by ethically sourced?

Here, in Omogi, we believe in ethical sourcing. We use environmentally friendly organic materials.
Omogi is open to everyone who wants to learn more about how we assess all social and environmental impact our business has.
Read more here.

I can design anything I like?

Yes! We produce seasonal and themed clothing and offers. We might have an Omogi month of Summer dresses or Omogi's October Halloween Fashion. So we might be selling only t-shirts atm but that doesn't mean you can't share your dress design. We'll make it once it's dresses time.
